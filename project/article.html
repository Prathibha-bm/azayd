Enabling ultra-personalized digital
experiences with AI

The global AI market in retail is set to skyrocket, growing from USD 7.14 billion in 2023 to an
impressive USD 85.07 billion by 2032. Gartner's 2023 Annual CIO and Technology Leaders Survey
reveals that nearly half of the industry leaders already leverage AI technologies in retail.

What's fueling this rapid growth? Is it robotics and automation, voice commerce, blockchain, or a
combination of these cutting-edge technologies? The acceleration of digital adoption during the
COVID-19 pandemic has pushed many brands to enhance their digital experiences. With the rise
of Generative AI, brands are poised to transform even further.

This blog explores how AI is revolutionizing the retail industry, delving into real-world
use cases in marketing and customer service. Join us as we uncover the
future of digital retail and the innovative technologies driving this transformation.
